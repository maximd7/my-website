Blocked by CSS Highlight Lock

Creepypasta.com – Story Pages
This site disables selection via user-select: none (and even blocks onselectstart).

Highlight Unlocker injects user-select: text!important and removes those handlers so you can click-and-drag to highlight.

Blocked by Clipboard-Replace Script

ClassicIndustries.com
This site installs a copy-event listener that replaces your selection with its own boilerplate text.

Highlight Unlocker intercepts that event, stops the site’s listener, and re-writes your highlighted text onto the clipboard.
